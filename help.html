Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Is FinanceU really free?
+
Yes! Our Free plan is completely free forever. You get access to Path 1 (Banking Basics) with 5 comprehensive lessons. No credit card required to sign up.
❓ Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
+
Absolutely. You can cancel your Pro or Expert subscription at any time from your account settings. There are no cancellation fees, and you'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
❓ Do I need any prior financial knowledge?
+
Not at all! FinanceU is designed for complete beginners. Our courses start with the absolute basics and build up gradually. If you can read this, you're qualified to start learning.
❓ How long does it take to complete a lesson?
+
Each lesson is designed to be completed in 10-20 minutes, but you can go at your own pace. Learn on your schedule - during breaks, between classes, or whenever works best for you.
❓ What payment methods do you accept?
+
We accept all major credit and debit cards through Stripe, our secure payment processor. We also support digital wallets like Apple Pay and Google Pay for quick checkout.
❓ Can I access FinanceU on mobile?
+
Yes! FinanceU is fully responsive and works great on all devices - desktop, tablet, and mobile. Learn anywhere, anytime from any device with an internet connection.
❓ Do you offer refunds?
+
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for first-time subscribers. If you're not satisfied within the first 14 days, contact us for a full refund. After 14 days, no refunds are provided for partial subscription periods.
❓ Is my personal information secure?
+
Absolutely. We take your privacy and security seriously. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and we never sell your personal information. Read our Privacy Policy for details.
