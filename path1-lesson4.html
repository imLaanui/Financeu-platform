Alex's Step-by-Step Guide:

  • 1. Compare Banks

    Go to NerdWallet or Bankrate. Look for accounts with 4%+ APY, $0 minimum balance, no monthly fees.

  • 2. Open Account (10 minutes)

    Click "Open Account" on the bank's website. Fill out the form with your info. They verify your identity instantly.

  • 3. Link Your Current Bank

    Enter your current bank's routing and account numbers (find these on your checks or banking app). Takes 2-3 days to verify.

  • 4. Transfer Your Savings

    Move your savings money (not your bill money). Keep enough in checking for daily expenses.

  • 5. Watch It Grow

    Check back in a month. You'll actually see interest adding up. It's addicting in the best way.
