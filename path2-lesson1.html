Avoid These Mistakes

Too Restrictive
Being too strict leads to burnout
Forgetting Irregulars

Ignoring Small Buys

Giving Up
One bad month doesn't mean failure

Your Action Plan

Complete this 10-minute exercise now:

List all income sources (salary, side gigs, etc.)
Write down fixed expenses (rent, insurance, subscriptions)
Estimate variable costs (groceries, gas, entertainment)
Calculate: Income - Expenses = ?
Identify one area to adjust if needed
