Choosing the Right Card

Different cards serve different purposes. Match the card to your needs:

  • Building Credit: Secured cards or student cards with no annual fee
  • Everyday Spending: Cash back cards (1.5-2% back on everything)
  • Specific Categories: Cards with bonus rewards on gas, groceries, or dining
  • Travel: Cards with travel points, no foreign transaction fees, travel insurance
  • Large Purchases: 0% intro APR cards (12-18 months interest-free)

üéØ For Beginners

Start with a simple cash back card with no annual fee. Once you've proven you can pay in full every month for 12+ months, then consider specialty rewards cards.

üéØ Your Smart Credit Card Strategy

Follow these rules to use credit cards safely:

Only charge what you can afford to pay off in full this month
Set up automatic payments to pay in full every month (or at least the minimum)
Keep credit utilization under 30% of your limit
Review statements monthly for errors or fraud
Never pay for rewards - if annual fee exceeds rewards value, it's not worth it
