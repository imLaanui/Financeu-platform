Hey, I'm the founder of FinanceU

A college finance major who got tired of watching my friends (and myself) struggle with money because no one ever taught us how it actually works.

My Story

Photo Coming Soon

I used to be terrified of anything money-related. Credit cards? No idea. Investing? Sounded like gambling. Budgeting? I tried apps that overcomplicated everything.

When I got to college, I realized I wasn't alone. My friends were getting into credit card debt, missing out on compound interest, and making financial mistakes that would follow them for years. Not because they were irresponsible, but because no one ever taught us this stuff.

So I decided to major in finance. And as I learned more, I got frustrated. All this information was scattered across random YouTube videos, Reddit threads, and textbooks written for Wall Street, not for students trying to figure out their first credit card.

That's why I built FinanceU. One platform. Simple lessons. Everything you actually need to know about money, explained the way I wish someone had explained it to me. No guru nonsense, no get-rich-quick schemes. Just the real system, broken down so any college student can understand it.

What Makes FinanceU Different

My Mission

To give every college student the financial education that school never provided. So you can graduate knowing how to actually manage money, build credit, invest for your future, and avoid the mistakes I almost made.
