LESSON 5

Cutting Expenses Without Sacrifice

Screen 1 of 8

Why Smart Expense Cutting Matters

(!)

The Problem

Cutting expenses feels like giving up fun and quality of life

The Solution

Small cuts add up fast without hurting your happiness

Your Goal

Eliminate waste, not joy - save hundreds per month

Screen 2 of 8

Smart Expense Cutting Strategies

Focus on eliminating waste, not happiness

Negotiate Bills
Save $20-50/month with one call
Generic Brands
Same quality, 20-40% less
Meal Prep
Save $80-150/week
Free Entertainment
Libraries, parks, museums
Energy Efficiency
Reduce bills by 10-30%

The key: Cut what you won't miss, keep what brings genuine value.

Avoid These Mistakes

Cutting Essentials
Don't sacrifice health or insurance
Complete Deprivation
No fun leads to binge spending
Not Comparing Prices
10 minutes saves 10-30%
Only Small Cuts
Focus on big recurring bills first
Screen 7 of 8
Your Action Plan

Complete these smart savings steps:

Call one service provider to negotiate your bill
Try one generic brand product instead of name brand
Plan and prep one meal for the week
Review last month's spending for mindless purchases
Identify one expense that brings zero joy to cut
Quick Reflection
Which expenses bring you joy vs. habit? The goal is to cut the mindless spending while keeping what truly enriches your life.
